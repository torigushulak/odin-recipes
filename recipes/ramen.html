Ramen

Description

Ramen is primarily a noodle and broth dish with assorted toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pork/Chicken broth
  • Ramen/Udon noodles
  • Bok choy
  • Halved egg

Steps

  1. Make broth of your choice
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Steam bok choy
  4. Boil egg and halve it
  5. Put other toppings to your liking
  6. Add all ingredients to a noice bowl
