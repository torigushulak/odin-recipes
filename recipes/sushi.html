Sushi

Description

Sushi comes in a variety of shapes and forms but most sushi has rice, seaweed, and some kind of filling/topping

Ingredients

  • sushi rice
  • seaweed paper
  • salmon/tuna/shrimp
  • sesame seeds
  • soy sauce
  • wasabi

Steps

  1. make sushi rice into oval shapes
  2. lay seafood of your choice on top of rice and squeeze
  3. place a seaweed paper strip across and ensure it sticks to rice
  4. pour soy sauce and add a pinch of sesame seeds
  5. Eat dat shit
